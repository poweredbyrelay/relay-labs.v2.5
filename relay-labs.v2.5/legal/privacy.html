This Policy applies to personal information processed by Relay Labs through its software-as-a-service (SaaS) platform, AI automation consulting, telecommunications integrations, websites, and related offerings (collectively, the "Services"). This Policy applies to all users globally, including users located in jurisdictions with enhanced privacy rights such as the European Union, the United Kingdom, and California.

2. Information We Collect

We may collect the following categories of personal information:

Identifiers and Contact Information: name, business email, business phone number, and account credentials.

Billing and Transaction Data: billing details and payment information, processed through Stripe.

Communications Content and Logs: message content, call detail records, SMS logs, metadata, and usage records transmitted through Twilio or other providers.

Technical and Usage Data: device identifiers, browser type, operating system, IP address, location approximations, referral URLs, pages visited, features used, and time spent within the Services.

Cookies and Tracking Data: collected via cookies, pixels, and similar technologies for functionality, analytics, and security.

We do not intentionally collect sensitive categories of personal information such as health data, biometric identifiers, or financial account numbers.
