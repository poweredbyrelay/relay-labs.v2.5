About Relay Labs

Streamlining operations and automating growth for modern businesses. We eliminate inefficiency and give companies leverage through intelligent automation.

Who We Are

Relay Labs is a consulting and SaaS solutions company built to streamline operations and automate growth for modern businesses. Our systems eliminate inefficiency, connect workflows, and give companies leverage through automation that scales with your ambitions.

Our Story

Founded by two brothers, Relay Labs started with a simple goal: create tools that make running a business less complicated. What began as a shared project quickly grew into a company serving clients who value speed, efficiency, and clarity in their operations.

What We Do

We integrate AI-driven automation with communication, lead management, and client engagement tools. Our platform is designed to give small and mid-sized businesses the same level of operational leverage that larger organizations rely on, without the enterprise complexity.
